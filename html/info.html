Welcome to the Pokemon Battle Game!

How to Play:

1. Choose two Pokemon: You need to select two Pokemon to battle against each other.

2. Battle Begins: Once you've selected the Pokemon, the battle will begin.

3. Attack: Click on the attack buttons to make your Pokemon attack the opponent.

4. Win or Lose: The battle continues until one of the Pokemon's HP reaches zero. If your Pokemon defeats the opponent, you win!

About the Game:

The game fetches data about the selected Pokemon from the PokeAPI to display their stats and images.

Each Pokemon has three stats displayed on the screen: HP, Attack, and Defense. HP represents the health points, while Attack and Defense determine the power of their attacks and their ability to withstand damage, respectively.

During the battle, you can choose from a set of attacks for your Pokemon to perform. Each attack has a certain power and accuracy. Use them strategically to defeat your opponent!

Game Mechanics:

  • The battle ends when one of the Pokemon's HP drops to zero.
  • If your Pokemon wins, you'll receive a victory message.
  • If your Pokemon loses, you'll receive a defeat message.

Have Fun!

Enjoy playing the Pokemon Battle Game and may the best Pokemon win!
